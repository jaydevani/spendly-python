Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects only the information necessary to provide the service: your name, email address, and the expense records you create (amount, category, date, and optional notes). We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to display your expense history and summaries within the app. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the service for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server running this application. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but this is a personal project and is not intended for storing sensitive financial information. You are responsible for the security of your deployment.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly loads fonts from Google Fonts. This means your browser will make a request to Google's servers when you visit the app. No other third-party services receive your personal data. We do not use any external analytics or tracking tools.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the project's repository or contact the administrator of this Spendly instance directly.
